Sewerage & Water Board of New Orleans

We thank Executive Director Ghassan Korban, Communications Director Richard Rainey, General Superintendent Bob Turner, and Drainage Supervisor Gerald Tilton for providing access to the drainage pump stations and the opportunity to speak with pump station operators. We’re grateful to each of the pump station operators we spoke with and observed in the summer and fall of 2019. They helped us understand how the pumps work, as well as key aspects of their lives as pump station operators.
